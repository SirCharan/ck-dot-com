## Making Trading Cool & Fun

## [Stocky Terminal](https://terminal.stockyai.xyz/)

India's first global OSINT dashboard. Tracks every news headline, twitter & video feed for crypto, commodities & stock market.

Along with map view for all of India's infra - ports, railways, refineries, power grids and trade routes, all in 1 place.

Can track earthquakes, monsoons, war updates, FED news, macro all in 1 place.

Makes trading cool, imagine 4 screen setups, people tracking every event, trading commodities 12+ hours (Industry like feeling at home with your friends)

![Stocky Terminal](/images/stocky/stocky-terminal.png)

## [Stocky LLM](https://stocky-ai.vercel.app)

![Stocky AI](/images/stocky/stocky-llm-compare.png)

Fine tuned models (Deepseek V3, Qwen3(multi-lingual)) with full focus on indian stock market and commodities. Access to real time data pipelines on block deals, bulk deals, supply chain disruptions etc from data sources built for Stocky Terminal.

![Stocky AI quick actions](/images/stocky/stocky-llm-buttons.png)

Simple 1 button click for most requested features + backtesting tools, connect to Zerodha for portfolio analysis and live trading.

## [Stocky Fun](https://stocky-fun.vercel.app/)

Bringing LLM intellegence into live video,real time vision APIs with faster than human eye response speeds. YC companies are building AI for security, OCR, sports.

I just thought lets make it a consumer use case.

Imagine putting a prompt for AI to give buy/sell signals based on what happening on the chart.

Will convert this to a high adrenaline, gamified UX and let people trade.

![Stocky Fun](/images/stocky/stocky-fun-trading.png)

Imagine replacing a 15 min insta scrolling session with 2-3 quick trades. (reduce time of trade and absolute PnL, increase overall brokerage)

## [Stocky Analyse](https://analyse.stockyai.xyz)
