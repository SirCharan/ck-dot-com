## June 2025

Claude launched its MCP Server. Game changer for me. Fine-tuned a claude 3.0 model and gave it 15L to trade the market.

Made 3.6L by september, 90k a month on auto-pilot. 3 weekly expiries (Bank Nifty on Wednesday, Nifty on Thursday and Sensex on Friday). 4 months. 51 trading days. Zero Losses. Felt invincible.

## Oct 2025 - Dec 2025

Hit my first loss on 29th September. Hurt. But didn't give up. Checked the logs. Was trading too many volatile stocks (Eternal, Asian Paints, Tata Motors, BSE).

Bad news hit - 3 weekly expiries (my bread and butter) was going to be lost. Bank Nifty shifted to a monthly expiry,

Changed up lots of stuff, explored Deepseek. October was a blast again. Faced my second loss in November.

Closed first 6 months of Stocky at 5.32L PnL, 212 profitable days, 2 loss days
